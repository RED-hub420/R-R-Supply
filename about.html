About

Local supply counter for PVF and fencing.

R&R Pipe and Supply handles everyday material needs in Wichita Falls with PVF stock, fencing products, shop prep, and local pickup or delivery support.

Most customers are not looking for a brochure. They need to know whether material is available, what information to send, and whether the order needs shop work or delivery.

This site keeps that process simple. Review common stock lines, build a material list, and send the sizes, quantities, and job notes the office needs to work the request.

What moves through the counter

Pipe, valves, fittings, flanges, studs, gaskets, wire, posts, gates, and other common line items for plant, shop, field, and ranch work.

What helps the office quote

Item type, size, rating or schedule, quantity, needed-by date, pickup or delivery, and any cutting, threading, or grooving notes.

How customers use the site

Pull together a clean list first, then call or send the request so stock, price, and timing can be checked in one pass.
