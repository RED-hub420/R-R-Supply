About

Local supply counter feel, cleaned up for web use.

R&R Pipe and Supply serves Wichita Falls with stocked PVF material, fencing supply, and practical shop support such as pipe cutting, threading, grooving, and local delivery coordination.

This site is organized around how customers usually buy: they need to confirm stock, review common categories, send sizes and quantities, and note whether the job needs will-call pickup, delivery, or custom prep.

Instead of writing like an ad, the site is built to feel like an order desk: clear categories, visible contact information, and a straightforward quote flow.

What is stocked

Pipe, valves, fittings, flanges, studs, gaskets, hardware, and fencing material set up in a way customers can actually scan.

Who it is for

Contractors, maintenance crews, fabrication shops, ranch buyers, and local customers who need material and service support in one place.

How requests move

Browse items, add quantities to the quote basket, send notes, and let the counter confirm availability, delivery, and any custom work needed.
